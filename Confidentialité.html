Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2025.

Welcome to G-Z, a web application dedicated to generating global demographic statistics. We respect and protect your privacy. This privacy policy describes the types of information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information Collected

a) Information Provided by Users

We collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Profession

b) Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect the following data:

  • City and country (based on your IP address)
  • Preferences stored in cookies for up to one year
  • Activity data, such as the number of shares

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To generate personalized content, such as recommendations based on your age and profession.
  • To analyze demographic trends and improve the platform.

3. Data Protection and Security

Your data is securely stored and protected through:

  • Encryption: Communications between your device and our servers are encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Secure Hosting: Data is hosted on Google Cloud servers, which comply with international data protection standards.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies to store your preferences for up to one year and local storage to enable you to resume activity where you left off.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data manually during an active session. Once the session expires, your data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to you.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be communicated through the application.
