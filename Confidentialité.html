Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3/9/2025.

Welcome to G-Z, a web application dedicated to generating global demographic statistics. We respect and protect your privacy. This privacy policy describes the types of information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information Collected

When using our application, you may voluntarily submit the following information:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Email address
  • Country of birth
  • Gender

2. Use of Information

We use the information you provide exclusively for:

  • Application authentication and user management.
  • Analyzing and generating anonymous statistics on demographics (by day, month, year, country, gender, age, etc.).
  • Providing updates and information regarding the application.

Your data will never be used for marketing, advertising, or other purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties, except in the following strictly necessary cases:

  • Service Providers: We use Google Cloud (Firebase) to host your data and PayPal to manage donations. These partners are also committed to maintaining strict privacy standards.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may be obligated to disclose your data to meet legal obligations.

Outside of these cases, your data is neither sold, rented, nor exchanged with anyone.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected by rigorous measures, including:

  • Encryption: All communications between your device and our servers are secured via SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Secure Hosting: Your data is stored on Google Cloud's secure servers, located in the United States, which comply with international data protection standards.

We also ensure that:

  • Submitted data is immediately aggregated and anonymized.
  • No information is traceable to an individual user.

5. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • After submission, a reset icon appears, allowing you to delete your data at any time as long as your session remains active.
  • Once your session is closed or expires, your data is irreversibly anonymized and can no longer be deleted.

We retain this aggregated data for long-term research and analysis. However, in case of a major issue or at your request, we commit to deleting all collected data.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We do not use any cookies or tracking technologies.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored in the United States, where our servers are located. We ensure that all data is processed in accordance with international data protection laws and with the same levels of security.

8. Retention Period

We retain anonymized data indefinitely to ensure its usefulness for future demographic analyses. However, no identifiable data is kept beyond the user's active session.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any significant changes will be communicated via our application or by email.

10. Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us:
