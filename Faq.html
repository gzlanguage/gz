FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Flower and what is it used for?

Flower is a web-based product of G-Z designed for personal development. By utilizing users' date of birth and profession, it generates personalized motivations across 165 categories.

)> About Flower

Throughout our lives, we encounter difficulties. However, your birthday can define you. Flower is not controlled by any person—it generates individual motivation based on your birthday and profession. It serves as a powerful mood lifter.

Please note that Flower is not associated with any religion, tradition, or other beliefs.

Updates: We are working to integrate different types of motivations—encouraging, playful, sad, motivating, and more—to enhance your experience.

3. Are my data secure?

Yes, your data is completely secure. We designed Flower to ensure that:

  • No data can be traced back to an individual user.
  • We do not collect any information that identifies who submitted what. Rest assured, your identity is protected and your data is immediately aggregated.
